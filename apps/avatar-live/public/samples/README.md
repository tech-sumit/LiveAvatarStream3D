# Sample newscasts

Ready-to-load demo documents for the studio (the **Load** menu reads these), plus their slide imagery and music bed.

> ⚠️ **All sample newscasts are fictional.** They were written purely to exercise the pipeline — emotion/gesture direction, camera presets, wall slides, tickers, music. Any resemblance to real news is the point of the *format*, not the *content*: the stories are invented, and this project is not affiliated with or endorsed by any company mentioned in them. The demo studio brand "SuVi AI News" is likewise fictional.
